# Bittensor Ecosystem & Resources

## Key Organizations
*   **Opentensor Foundation (OTF)**: The core team responsible for protocol development, network maintenance, and governance (TUMs/BITs).
*   **The Bittensor Community**: A decentralized group of developers, researchers, and subnet teams.

## Major Explorers & Dashboards
*   **[TAOstats.io](https://taostats.io)**: The most widely used block explorer. Tracks block production, transfers, and subnet metrics.
*   **[TAO.app](https://www.tao.app)**: Excellent for tokenomics analysis, subnet discovery, and validator comparison.
*   **[Subnet Explorer](https://subnets.bittensor.com)**: Official dashboard for monitoring subnet performance.

## Essential Documentation
*   **Official Docs**: [docs.learnbittensor.org](https://docs.learnbittensor.org) (The gold standard for technical guides).
*   **Whitepaper**: [bittensor.com/whitepaper](https://bittensor.com/whitepaper).
*   **Subnets Repo**: [github.com/opentensor/subnets](https://github.com/opentensor/subnets).

## Subnet Teams to Watch
*   **Affine**: Working on SN 120.
*   **Foundry**: Major validator and infrastructure provider.
*   **Corcel**: A front-end interface (Chat/Search) powered by Bittensor subnets.
