# Bittensor Core Vision & Architecture

## Core Vision
Bittensor's primary mission is to **direct the power of digital markets toward Artificial Intelligence**. 

Unlike centralized AI development where a few large corporations control the models and data, Bittensor aims to:
*   Decentralize the ownership and benefits of machine intelligence.
*   Allow "mere mortals" to participate in and benefit from the AI economy.
*   Create a bottom-up, incentivized framework for AI development.

## Architecture
Bittensor is a **decentralized machine learning protocol** that functions as a meta-layer for digital markets.

### Key Architectural Pillars:
1.  **The Meta-Protocol**: Bittensor provides an abstraction for digital markets, similar to how Ethereum provided an abstraction for decentralized contracts.
2.  **Subnets (Commodity Markets)**: The network consists of numerous independent subnets, each specializing in a specific digital commodity (e.g., text-to-image, storage, compute, protein folding).
3.  **Unified Token System**: All subnets operate under the same currency ($TAO), creating a shared economic ecosystem.
4.  **Miners & Validators**: 
    *   **Miners**: Perform the actual work (e.g., training a model, providing storage).
    *   **Validators**: Evaluate the work produced by miners and reach consensus on its value.
5.  **Incentive Mechanisms**: Subnet creators design the rules for how validators evaluate miners, ensuring that the network rewards the most valuable contributions.

### Abstraction Layer
Bittensor abstracts the complexity of blockchain and incentive design, allowing developers to focus on the "commodity" (the AI/ML task) rather than the underlying consensus mechanics.
