# Bittensor Tokenomics ($TAO)

## Token Specifications
*   **Symbol**: TAO (τ)
*   **Maximum Supply**: 21,000,000 TAO (Same as Bitcoin)
*   **Current Circulating Supply**: ~10.85M TAO (April 2026)
*   **Emission Rate**: 1 TAO is emitted per block (~every 12 seconds).

## Emission & Halving
*   **Daily Emission**: 7,200 TAO is distributed daily across the entire network.
*   **Halving Model**: TAO follows a 4-year halving cycle, mimicking Bitcoin’s scarcity model.
*   **Next Halving**: Estimated in approximately **3 years and 9 months**.

## Distribution Mechanics
TAO emissions are distributed to four main groups of participants:
1.  **Miners**: Rewarded for producing high-quality work in subnets.
2.  **Validators**: Rewarded for accurately evaluating miner work and securing the consensus.
3.  **Subnet Creators**: Rewarded for building and maintaining valuable incentive mechanisms.
4.  **Stakers**: Token holders who delegate TAO to validators receive a portion of the validator rewards.

## Staking & Security
*   **Staking Ratio**: Approximately **67%** of the circulating supply is currently staked (April 2026).
*   **Delegated Staking**: Users can stake their TAO to any active validator to earn a passive APY.
*   **Slashing**: While native slashing is minimal, the "economic slashing" comes from low evaluation scores which results in reduced emissions for poor performers.

## Utility
*   **Governance**: Voting on network-wide proposals (TUMs/BITs).
*   **Registration**: Paying for subnet slots and miner/validator identities (hotkeys).
*   **Medium of Exchange**: TAO is the unit of account for all AI services produced on the network.
